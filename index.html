Bill Splitter

How to use this:
  1. Fill in the number of people the bill is split among.

  2. Fill in the total nett bill amount (i.e. inclusive of any service charge and/or GST).

  3. Add individual items that are not being shared among the group. (E.g. Person X ordered an individual dessert.)

    If this amount already includes GST and/or service charge, DO NOT tick the checkboxes for GST and service charge.

    If you need to add GST and/or service charge, tick the relevant boxes accordingly.

  4. Click 'Calculate' to display how much each person should pay.

  5. Fill in payment method. Screenshot and show everyone in your group or send in your group chat!
